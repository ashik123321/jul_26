University of Barishal at a glance

Barisal University Rover Scout Group started its journey from 10 November 2013 with 2 (two) rover units and 1 (one) Girl-in-Rover unit. Two more rover units have been added on 16 May 2016. The unit leaders of the rover units are: Mr. Md. Mahiuddin Sabbir, Mr. Md. Imran Hossain, Mr. Md. Arif Hosen, Mr. Md. Arham Sayeed. The unit leader of Girl-in-Rover unit is: Marzia Nomi. The assistant unit leaders of Girl-in-Rover unit are: Dilafroze Khanam, Fatema-Tuz-Zohora and Sumona Rani Saha.
